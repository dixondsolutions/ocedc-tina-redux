      **Vision:** *"Ogle County, Illinois: An economic destination for growth

      ## Our Story


      * 2017: County and municipal leaders form an ad hoc committee to
      coordinate development efforts. - 2019: Utilities, higher education, and
      private investors formalize their partnership. - 2023: OCEDC incorporates
      as a public–private corporation with county-wide coverage. - Today: We

      partners, and site selectors nationwide.

      responses. - Strategic planning, BRE (Business Retention & Expansion), and
      community marketing. - Access to utility, transportation, state, and
      workforce development partners.
