## What the grant funds

- Extension of water, sewer, and fiber along Steward Road and Johnstown Road.
- Heavy power upgrades to deliver 100+ MW for advanced manufacturing.
- Dual Class I rail improvements, including new switches and a transload pad.

## Why it matters

The Rochelle Rail Campus already offers 160 acres of certified industrial land, direct access to I-39/I-88, and proximity to Global III intermodal. The new infrastructure package will shorten project timelines by 12–18 months.

## Next steps

OCEDC is coordinating with site selectors and existing industries to market the upgraded mega-site. Contact **madison@ocedc.org** for technical data or to schedule a tour.
