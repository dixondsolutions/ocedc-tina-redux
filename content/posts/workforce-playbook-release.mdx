### Inside the playbook

1. **Career pathways** for welding, mechatronics, CDL, food safety, and data center operations.
2. **Training incentives** that stack WIOA, EDGE, and utility grants.
3. **Recruitment resources** including talent attraction marketing assets for employers.

### How employers can use it

- Align job descriptions with credential-ready graduates.
- Apply for incumbent-worker training dollars with turnkey templates.
- Plug into shared equipment labs and apprenticeship cohorts.

Email **talent@ocedc.org** to request the PDF or on-campus presentation.
