### Key takeaways

- **$92 million** in active projects spanning manufacturing, logistics, housing, and downtown redevelopment.
- Launch of the **OCEDC Sites & Buildings portal** powered by TinaCMS for real-time editing.
- Introduction of a **marketing co-op** that helps communities share photo/video assets across campaigns.

### Action items for investors

1. Submit project leads directly through the contact form for concierge support.
2. Share upcoming property listings or BRE intel with OCEDC staff.
3. Attend quarterly governance meetings rotating among member communities.

Thank you to Byron Bank, ComEd, Nicor, and the City of Oregon for sponsoring the roundtable.
